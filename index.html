Travel Tracker

Travel Tracker is an Artificial Intelligence planner that is designed to track the best travel
methods for you to get where you want to go in the most cost efficient manner.

What would set travel tracker apart is that its ability to monitor travel prices across the sky, over the road,
or along the rail! While geared more toward the European continent, this can still be used within the United States as well!

Nothing is more frustrating currently than wanting to take a trip to Europe but the cost of tickets to your location
is way more than you have. But what if you could fly to another location for half the price and then take a train to your location?

This would maximize your exposure to locations visited while staying under your budget. Travel Tracker would monitor and provide cost
updates along the way of your trip and depending on the time of day, could even offer up hotel or hostels to stay at.
